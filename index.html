I am a Staff Research Engineer at Google DeepMind, focusing on Generative AI research and productionization. My work centers on Gemini 2.0 and 2.5+, specifically advancing multimodal post-training, data efficiency in SFT and RL, and developing new model capabilities tailored for product use cases.

More importantly, I lead an Applied AI team dedicated to bridging the 'last mile' between core models and customer expectations. By collaborating with researchers, engineers, PMs, and stakeholders, we have successfully delivered numerous end-to-end GenAI solutions for large-scale production.

Before GDM, I was a machine learning software engineer at YouTube Search and a research assistant at the Institute for Security, Technology, and Society working with Prof.VS Subrahmanian.

My research interests include data mining, data efficiency and multimodal understanding.
